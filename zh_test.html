Hanja highlighter

How to use

Paste text into the box below. The difficulty levels of any kanji included (according to the Kanji Kentei, at least) will be represented by use of a color gradient. Hover over any kanji to see its corresponding numerical grade.

  • If the conversion option is enabled, the site will try to convert the text into a more Japanese-friendly form by substituting Japanese character forms where possible. (Click one of the samples below to try it out!)
  • The monochrome color palette is typically more readable.
